How To Bathe a Cat

  1. Fill the bathtub with warm water, to the height of the legs of the cat
  2. Place a towel on the bottom of the bathtub for the cat to grip on
  3. Put the cat in the tub
  4. Get the cat wet
  5. Lather the cat up with soap
  6. Hold down the cat so it doesn't jump out
  7. Rinse off the soap
  8. Dry the cat with a towel
  9. Blow dry the cat with a hair dryer on low heat
  10. Done! Hope you got out unscathed
