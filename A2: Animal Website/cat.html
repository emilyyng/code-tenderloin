How To Bathe a Cat

  1. Fill the bathtub with warm water, to the height of the legs of the cat
  2. Place a towel on the bottom of the bathtub for the cat to grip on
  3. Put the cat in the tub
  4. Lather the cat up with soap
  5. Rinse off the soap
  6. Dry the cat with a towel
  7. Blow dry the cat with a hair dryer on low heat
